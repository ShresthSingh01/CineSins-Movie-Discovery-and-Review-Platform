## Personality Quick Test (5 Questions)

1. **Reality Check**
   * Question: Do you prefer stories grounded in gritty realism or whimsical fantasy?
   * Option A: Gritty Realism (Increases `cognitiveLoad` tolerance)
   * Option B: Whimsical Fantasy (Increases `comfortScore` bias)

2. **The Pace**
   * Question: How do you feel about "slow-burn" movies that take their time?
   * Option A: Love the depth. (Increases `cognitiveLoad` score)
   * Option B: Get to the point! (Increases `comfortScore` bias)

3. **Emotional Depth**
   * Question: Are you looking for a movie that will make you cry or feel intense emotion?
   * Option A: Yes, give me the feels. (Increases `emotionalIntensity` score)
   * Option B: No, I want to relax. (Increases `comfortScore` bias)

4. **Plot Structure**
   * Question: Do you enjoy complex, non-linear plots that require focused attention?
   * Option A: Absolutely, I love puzzles. (Increases `cognitiveLoad` score)
   * Option B: I prefer straightforward stories. (Increases `comfortScore` bias)

5. **Visual Experience**
   * Question: What matters more: stunning visual spectacle or thought-provoking subtext?
   * Option A: Visual Spectacle (Increases `emotionalIntensity` score)
   * Option B: Thought-provoking Subtext (Increases `cognitiveLoad` score)
